Go Back Mulan´s porridge

Ingredients

>- Rice
- 2 eggs
- Bacon
- Oil
- Salt

Step 1: In a pot pour a tablespoon of oil and half a cup of rice. Heat until the rice turns a golden color.


Step 2: Add a cup of boiling water. Cover the pot and leave it on low heat for 20 minutes. Ideally put a toaster underneath to prevent it from burning.


Step 3: After about 10 minutes from when the rice cooking began, pour oil into a pan and fry the bacon strips. (The preparation requires only one, but cooking to the taste of the diner). Once they acquire a caramel color, remove from the pan and leave on a separate plate.


Step 4:In the same pan, pour two eggs and fry them carefully not to break the yolks


Step 5: When the rice is ready, serve to taste in a bowl or plate and put on top the two fried eggs as "eyes" and bacon as a "smile"..


Step 6: Enjoy.
