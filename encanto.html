Go Back Arepas from Encanto

Ingredients

- 4 cups or 500g precooked white cornmeal
- 4 cups or 400g of mozzarella cheese blend
- 1 cup 100g cotija cheese
- A bit of salt
- 3 1/2 cups or 800ml warm water
- 1 1/4 cup or 150g unsalted butter (melted)

Step 1:In a large bowl, whisk together the cornmeal, slat, cotija cheese and 2 cups of the mozzaralla cheese.


Step 2:Stir in the water and melted butter to form a soft dough. It should be the consistency of homemade playdough.


Step 3:Cover the bowl with a kitchen towel and allow it to sit for 10 minutes.


Step 4:Knead the dough for a few minutes until it is smooth and soft. It should hold its shape, but be easily worked.


Step 5:Divide the dough into 16 equal balls.


Step 6:Pat each ball of dough into a 5 inch circle that is about ¼” thick.


Step 7:Place a few tablespoons of the remaining shredded cheese in the middle of the patty.


Step 8:Sandwich another piece of dough over it and seal the edges by pinching. Seal well.


Step 9:Pat the dough into a patty that is about 3 inches wide and a ½” thick.


Step 10:Repeat with remaining balls of dough. Set aside.


Step 11:Heat a 12” cast iron skillet over medium high heat or an electric griddle at 160°.


Step 12:When the skillet is hot, place a small pad of butter on the skillet and spread it around to coat the surface.


Step 13:Place the arepas on the skillet and cook for 5 minutes, until crispy and golden


Step 14:Flip and cook on the second side for 5 minutes.


Step 15:Transfer to a wire rack to cool while you continue cooking the rest. Keep in a warm oven


Step 16:Enjoy.
