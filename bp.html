Go Back Bacon Pancakes

Ingredients

- 6 strips bacon(or more)
- 128g flour
- 26g white sugar
- 25g brown sugar
- 4g baking powder
- 177-237 ml milk
- 1 egg
- 15ml oil
- 5ml vanilla
- 90g chocolate chips
- Maple syrup

Step 1:Cook bacon by frying over stovetop until crisp. Set aside.


Step 2:In a medium bowl, combine dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder).


Step 3:In another bowl, combine egg, oil, and vanilla. Once thoroughly mixed, add to bowl with dry ingredients.


Step 4:Add milk, stirring well until the mixture resembles batter. If too dry, add more milk.


Step 5:Heat frying pan with oil, and pour on pancake batter. Top with bacon and chocolate chips.


Step 6:Once pancake has set, flip and continue to cook until golden brown on both sides.


Step 7:Transfer to plate. Drizzle with maple syrup.
