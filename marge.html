Go Back Marge’s Dessert Dogs

Ingredients

- 2 cups of flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup or 60g butter
- 1/2 cup or 50g brown sugar
- 1/2 cup or 65g of mayonnaise
- 3/4 cup or 100g of granulated sugar
- 3 egg whites
- 3/4 cup or 100g fine sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1 cup of cherries
- 1/2 cup or 65g sugar
- 1 cup or 130g sugar
- 6 tbsp butter
- 1/2 cup or 65g heavy cream

Step 1:Mix flour and baking soda together in a large bowl, set aside.


Step 2:Beat together butter with granulated and brown sugar, until fluffy


Step 3:, add the eggs and mix well, followed by the vanilla extract.


Step 4:Finally, slowly add the flour mixture until combined. Do not over mix


Step 5:Roll cookie dough into a hot dog shape and bake until firm, then deep fry into done.



Meringue Bun:


Step 1:Heat the oven to 110 degrees and line the baking sheet with parchment paper.


Step 2:Thoroughly separate the egg whites and whisk in a stand mixer with cream of tartar on medium speed.


Step 3:Slowly start adding the sugar until the mixture forms a foam. Then increase the speed and continue adding the sugar until soft peaks form.


Step 4:Then continue the mixer running on high speed until firm peaks form – that is when the peaks hold their shape and are glossy.


Step 5:Shape the meringue into a “bun” shape and bake for approximately 1 hour or until soft and firm. Let the bun cool in the oven completely with the oven door open.



Cherry Ketchup:


Step 1:Using a food processor, combine the cherries and sugar until well combined


Step 2:. Reduce the mixture on low heat on the stove in a small pan until the mixture reaches a more ketchup-like consistency.



Caramel Mustard


Step 1:Pour sugar into a saucepan and heat on medium/high heat until it is melted. Keep a close eye on the sugar or it will burn!


Step 2:When the sugar turns a light brown colour immediately turn off the heat and add the cream and whisk until combined. Next, add the butter and continue stirring


Step 3:Allow the mixture to cool and the sauce will thicken as it does.


Step 4:: assemble the meringue bun first on a plate. Next place the cookie onto the bun, followed by drizzling the cheery ketchup and caramel mustard.


Step 5:Enjoy
