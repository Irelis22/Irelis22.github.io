Ingredients

- 150g ground beef (80 percent lean)
- 2 tsp. Old Bay seasoning
- 1 tsp. Black pepper
- 1 tsp. Sea salt
- 2 slices cheddar cheese
- 1/2 cup or 65g of mayonnaise
- 2 tsp. Old Bay seasoning
- Sesame seed buns, sliced in half
- Ketchup
- Mustard
- Pickle slices
- Red onion, sliced
- Tomato, sliced
- 2 leaves of butter lettuce

Step 1:Heat a grill pan or sauté pan over medium heat.


Step 2:As it heats, combine ground beef, Old Bay, pepper and salt.


Step 3:. Coat the warmed grill pan with cooking spray and place patties on the pan.


Step 4:cooking each side about 4-5 minutes, or until cooked through to your desired doneness.


Step 5:2. Use a large star-shaped cookie cutter to cut each cheese slice into a star shape.


Step 6:Place the star-shaped cheese on each burger patty, in the last 30 seconds or so of cooking, so it melts a little.


Step 7:Set burger patties aside.



Make King Neptune’s Poseidon Powder Aioli:


Step 1:Stir mayo and Old Bay until thoroughly combined.



Assemble the Sandw:


Step 1:Spread Poseidon Powder Aioli on the bottom of each bun.


Step 2:Top with a burger patty, ketchup, mustard, pickles, onion slices, tomato and lettuce.


Step 3:Place the top bun on each burger. .


Step 4:Serve.
