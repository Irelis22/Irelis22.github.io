Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons of honey
- 1 cup or 130g flour
- 2 eggs
- 2/3 cup or 80g sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 cup or 100g anko (sweet red bean paste) or nutella
- 50 ml cream
- 50 ml water
- Pinch of salt

Step 1:In a large bowl, whisk eggs and sugar until it becomes fluffy.


Step 2:Add baking soda, honey and water. Mix well


Step 3:Gradually mix the flour in the egg mixture until it is a thick pancake better. Cover the bowl with plastic and leave it for 15-20 min.


Step 4:Heat the frying pan over medium heat, the pan should be greased with oil.


Step 5:Pour the batter in the pan to create 3 inch diameter pancake. When the bubbles appear on the surface flip it over and cook the other side.


Step 6:Gently mix cream, 1/2 tablespoon sugar and red bean paste or the nutella.


Step 7:Sandwich two pancakes with red bean paste mixture and serve warm. (Note: Cook these pancakes on low heat because honey added in the batter will burn easily).
