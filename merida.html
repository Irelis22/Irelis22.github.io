Go Back Cookies from Brave

Ingredients

- 2-3 cups or 85 g flour (depending on how sticky the batter is)
- 3/4 cup or 100 g sugar
- 1/2 cup or 65 g butter
- 1/4 cup or 240 ml whole milk
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 1/2 cup or 65 g confectioners sugar
- 1 or 2 tablespoons whole milk

Step 1:Preheat oven to 350 degrees.


Step 2:In a stand mixer, cream together softened butter and sugar.


Step 3:Next add milk, followed by one egg at a time, and then vanilla extract


Step 4:Slowly add in flour mixture until well combined


Step 5:Drop cookie dough onto a baking sheet and bake 7-9 minutes


Step 6:Let cookies full cool before icing.


Step 7:Combine confectioners sugar with milk and mix until a not too runny consistency is formed.


Step 8:You might need to experiment by adding more milk or icing sugar until the right consistency is achieved.
