Go Back Tree Trunk´s apple pie

Ingredients

- 2 southern pie crust rounds
- 170 g butter sauce
- 4-8 apples(Granny Smith)
- 25 g brown sugar
- 2.64 g cinnamon
- 0.59 g nutmeg

Step 1:Preheat oven to 220 degrees.


Step 2:Roll out dough. .


Step 3:Place bottom crust in pan.


Step 4:Peel, core, and slice apples.


Step 5:Place apple slices in a large bowl.


Step 6:Toss with spices and lemon juice.


Step 7:Mound the apples in the crust.


Step8:Make and pour butter sauce (below) evenly over the apples.


Step9:Top with another crust layer. Cut slits in the top.


Step10:Bake 15 minutes. Reduce the temperature to 175 degrees, and continue baking until pie is done (about 40 minutes).
