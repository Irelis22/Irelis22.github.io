Go Back Naco

Ingredients

- Burrito-sized flour tortillas
- 1/4 cup melted butter
- 500g ground beef
- 1 packet taco seasoning and required water
- 1 can refried beans
- Corn tortilla chips
- 1 tomato, diced
- A few leaves of green leaf lettuce
- Sour cream
- 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1 cup of cherries
- 1/2 cup or 65g sugar
- 1 cup or 130g sugar
- 6 tbsp butter
- 1/2 cup or 65g heavy cream

Step 1:Preheat the oven to 180 degrees.


Step 2:Brown the meat in a large pan, and prepare it with the taco seasonings as indicated on the package. Make sure to break the meat up really well.


Step 3:Meanwhile, warm the refried beans and nacho cheese in separate pots. Break some tortilla chips into smaller pieces.


Step 4:Place a tortilla on a baking tray and brush it with melted butter


Step 5: Put a ramekin (or other small dish) in the center of the tortilla and gather the tortilla around it.


Step 6:Tie the folds in place with baking string and brush the whole tortilla with butter (make sure to get underneath, too).


Step 7:Bake the tortilla until the edges are golden brown and it's stiff (about 5-10 minutes).


Step 8: Remove the string and carefully pull out the ramekin.


Step 9:Fill the tortilla with nacho ingredients and dig in.
