Go Back Sandwich of Death

Ingredients

- 1 Cup or 225g Spaghetti Sauce or Pizza Sauce
- 1/2 Cup or 100g BBQ Sauce
- Split Top Sub Rolls, cut lengthwise
- Package of Sliced Sandwich Pepperoni
- Package of Small Sliced Canadian Bacon
- 500g. Ground Beef
- 500g. Ground Turkey
- 100g Prosciutto, chopped
- 1 Egg
- 1/4 Cup or 50g Shredded Parmesan
- 2 Tbsp. Plain Bread Crumbs
- 1 Tbsp. Fresh Italian Parsley, chopped
- Pinch of salt
- Pinch of Ground Black Pepper

Step 1:Preheat the oven to 180 degrees.


Step 2:Spray a large baking dish with non-stick spray. In a large bowl, use your hands to squish together all of the meatball ingredients until well combined


Step 3:Roll into balls (about ping pong size) & place them in the baking dish in a single layer.


Step 4:Cover with foil & bake for 25-30 minutes or until cooked through


Step 5:Remove the meatballs to a container & discard the juices from the baking tray.


Step 6:In a medium bowl, stir together the spaghetti or pizza sauce with the BBQ sauce, & then spread the mixture on the cut open sub roll.


Step 7:Fold several slices of sandwich pepperoni & Canadian bacon in half & lay them alternately across the bottom half of the sub roll.


Step 8:Top with a row of meatballs & optional extra shredded parmesan &/or chopped fresh parsley, & then close with the top half of the sub roll


Step 9:Eat wearing cut-off jeans & a mullet and DO NOT LEAVE SANDWICH UNACCOMPANIED UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCES.
